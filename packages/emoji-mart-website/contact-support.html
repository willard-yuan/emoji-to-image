Common Issues & Quick Fixes

Before contacting support, try these quick solutions to common problems:

🖼️ Download not working?

Try refreshing the page and selecting the emoji again. Make sure your browser allows downloads from our site and check your download settings. If using Chrome, ensure the download isn't being blocked by your security settings.

📋 Copy function not working?

Ensure your browser supports clipboard access. Try using keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+C or Cmd+C on Mac) after selecting the emoji. Some browsers require permission to access your clipboard - check your browser settings.

🔍 Search not finding emojis?

Try different keywords or browse by categories. Some emojis might have unexpected names or tags. For example, search for "happy" instead of "smile" or "face" instead of "emoji" to broaden your results. Try our main converter or copy & paste tool for different search experiences.

📱 Mobile display issues?

Make sure you're using an updated browser. Try clearing your browser cache and cookies. On iOS, try using Safari; on Android, Chrome often works best. If the site appears zoomed in, try double-tapping or pinching to adjust the view.
